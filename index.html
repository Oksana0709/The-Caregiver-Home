FAQ

1. What is the app for?
The app is for caregivers to find and connect with potential clients.
2. How does the app work?
Caregivers can create a profile on the app and search for potential clients
based on location, services needed, and other criteria. Once they find a client
they are interested in, they can contact them directly through the app.
3. How much does the app cost?
The app is free to download and use.
4. What are the requirements for using the app?
Caregivers must be 18 years or older and have a valid driver's license. They
must also pass a background check.
5. What are the benefits of using the app?
Caregivers can find potential clients quickly and easily, and they can
communicate with them directly through the app. The app also allows
caregivers to track their appointments and payments.
